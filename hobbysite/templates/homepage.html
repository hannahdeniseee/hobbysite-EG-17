ABOUT US

HobbySite is a project built by five Computer Science students who believe that hobbies deserve more than just a side panel on social media. Whether you're an artist, collector, gamer, or maker, we've built this space for you. Hobbysite has grown into a full-featured platform — complete with a blog, commissions marketplace, community forum, merch store, and wiki — designed to help hobbyists connect, share, and thrive.
